Monthly Challenge

Reduce your plastic waste!

1. Bring reusable bags to shop
2. Use reusable water bottles and mugs
3. Use reusable straws or say no to straws.

If not you are killing sea turtles!

Facts

5 trillion plastic bags produced yearly

5 trillion plastic bags are being produced yearly. The amount of plastic produced in one year is about the same as the entire weight of humanity

5.25 trillion pieces of plastic trash

There are 5.25 trillion pieces of plastic trash in the ocean. Each year, 7 billion kg is being added to the count

Many species are being affected

Ingestion and entanglement of marine debris affects over 267 animal species

Many animals die every year

Tens of thousands of sharks, rays, sea turtles, seabirds, seals, whales die every year from contact with ocean-borne plastic bags
